Competition

INTERNAL MATCHES

On week 5 of our program, all age groups will play 1-hour of small sided matches in every session. Teams will be mixed every 15 minutes so every child plays with and against each other.

FRIENDLY GAMES

Matches and tournaments will be scheduled throughout the season as this type of games gives every child a chance to sample the match day experience.

LEAGUE GAMES

Our Friday leagues, ranging from U7 to U16 for the more advanced players in each age group. All players in the academy will be monitored and given the chance to join the team, throughout the season.

Areas of Concern

COACHES

SFA doesn't depend on individual coaches as we have a well defined SFA coaching program in place that everyone follows. Our skilled coaches will help the child to get out of his comfort zone.

SPACE

Playing football in tight spaces is great for the development of your child. Players get lots of touches of the football, improving confidence, ball mastery and learn when to dribble and when to pass.

GAMES

Win, lose or draw at matches are vital for kids development in training we learn our system of play while in matches we put it to pratice. All players get a chance to be part of the team.

FITNESS

We include our fitness program with the ball so its more enjoyable, improving the football skills and the fitness at the same time. We want to develop football players, not field and track players.

Understanding The Techniques

KEEP THE BALL

This segment of our program teaches the children to keep the ball in a variety of different game related situations.

WIN THE BALL

It's vital for our players to understand that if they or their teammates lose the ball, they should naturally work hard to regain possession.

TRANSITION

Transition from attack to defence and defence to attack are the most crucial concept in football. What do we do when we lose the ball? How to react when we win the ball?

SHOOTING

Shooting with different parts of feet. We teach the correct techniques, body shape and spatial awareness involved in the art of shooting.
